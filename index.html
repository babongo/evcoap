What is it

Evcoap aims at providing a fully compliant implementation of the CoAP protocol, of its discovery companion Link-Format, and of the pretty useful Observe and Block options.

Evcoap is based on Libevent 2.x by Niels Provos and Nick Mathewson, and shares much of the design philosophy found in its evhttp front-end.

Goal

Our final goal is to give libevent users a robust set of tools for creating compliant CoAP-based applications including, but not limited to, HTTP-CoAP interworking proxies.
